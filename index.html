About Us

Delhi Public School was founded in 1995 with the aim of providing quality education to students in the Delhi region. Since then, we have expanded to multiple campuses across the country and have developed a reputation for excellence in education.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility. Our vision is to be a leading educational institution that prepares students for success in the global community.

History

Delhi Public School was founded by Mr. Ravi Kumar, a visionary entrepreneur who saw the need for quality education in the Delhi region. Under his leadership, the school has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the country.

Management

Delhi Public School is managed by a team of experienced educators and administrators who are committed to providing the best possible education to our students. Our management team is dedicated to ensuring that our students receive the support they need to succeed in their academic and personal lives.

Academics

At Delhi Public School, we offer a wide range of academic programs that cater to the needs of students from all backgrounds. Our curriculum is designed to foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity.

Primary School

Our primary school program focuses on developing foundational skills in reading, writing, and mathematics. We also offer classes in science, social studies, and art.

Middle School

In middle school, our students continue to build on their foundational skills while also exploring new subjects like foreign languages, computer science, and music.

High School

Our high school program prepares students for success in college and beyond. We offer a wide range of advanced courses in subjects like mathematics, science, literature, and history.
